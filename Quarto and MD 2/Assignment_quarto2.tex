\hypertarget{an-american-television-sitcom}{%
\subsection{An American television
sitcom}\label{an-american-television-sitcom}}

The Big Bang Theory is an American television sitcom created by
\textbf{Chuck Lorre} and \textbf{Bill Prady}, both of whom served as
executive producers on the series, along with Steven Molaro. The three
of them also served as head writers. It premiered on CBS on September
24, 2007, and concluded on May 16, 2019, having broadcast 279 episodes
over 12 seasons.
